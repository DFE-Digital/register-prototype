Assessment Only (AO) guidance

Assessment Only route

AO is a route to Qualified Teacher Status (QTS) in England for:

  • experienced teachers with a degree
  • people with teaching qualifications from other countries

It allows teachers to demonstrate that they already meet all the QTS standards without the need for further training.

Find out more about the AO route policy.

Timelines for AO candidate data

There are no timelines for the AO route. It is an assessment period of up to 12 weeks. You can register AO candidates at any stage during the academic year.

AO candidates do not form part of the census collection as they are already part of the workforce.
