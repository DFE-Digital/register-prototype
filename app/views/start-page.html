Use this service to:

  • register trainee data with the Department for Education, and keep it updated
  • get a teacher reference number (TRN) for your trainees
  • recommend your trainees for qualified teacher status (QTS) or early years teacher status (EYTS)

If you’re a higher education institution (HEI)

Use this service, instead of the Higher Education Statistics Agency (HESA), to register your trainees.

When to register your trainees

Register your trainees when they start their programme.

If you need to register a trainee who started in a previous academic year, get in touch with the data management team at the Department for Education at itt.datamanagement@education.gov.uk.
